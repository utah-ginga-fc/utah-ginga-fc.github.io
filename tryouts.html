Tryouts

Show us your ginga. Join Utah's competitive futsal club.

👥

Who Can Try Out?

We welcome high school-aged players (14-18) who are passionate about futsal and ready to compete. All skill levels are encouraged to attend.

⚡

What to Expect

Tryouts include small-sided games, technical drills, and positional play. We look for creativity, effort, and a love for the game above all.
