ABOUT US

It’s no coincidence that futsal is the first game many soccer superstars begin their journey with. The fast-paced nature of the game, combined with the high number of touches per minute, fosters accelerated growth in technical skills and creativity. These boys have developed a deep love for futsal and are passionate about promoting a sport that fits perfectly with Utah’s cold seasons.

Out of this passion, Utah Ginga was born—with a mission to offer quality futsal opportunities at very low costs. Through their creativity and determination, these boys have found innovative ways to keep the program affordable. However, they would greatly benefit from access to better practice facilities and occasional sessions with more experienced coaches. They work tirelessly to support their passion, but at this stage in their lives, most of their financial resources are dedicated to LDS missions and furthering their education.

If you feel inspired to support their cause and help them grow, here’s the link to make a donation. Your contribution can make a meaningful impact in helping these boys continue their futsal journey and share their love for the game with the community.
